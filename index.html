The Mother

The Mother (2023) is an action thriller film starring Jennifer Lopez. The story follows a former CIA operative, known only as "The Mother," who is forced into hiding to protect her daughter from a dangerous criminal organization. After years of isolation, she is thrust back into action when the criminals who threaten her daughter resurface. Despite her lack of contact with the outside world, The Mother is determined to use her lethal skills to save her child. The film blends high-stakes action with emotional drama as The Mother navigates her past and risks everything to protect her loved one. The movie is directed by Niki Caro and features intense action sequences and a central theme of motherhood and sacrifice.
